Welcome to Wellness Hub. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password
- **Profile Details:** Bio, profile picture, country, timezone
- **Preferences:** Dietary restrictions, measurement system, language, theme
- **Progress Data:** Food consumption logs, meal times, defense system tracking
- **Content:** Recipes, meal plans, shopping lists, pantry items
- **Interactions:** Favorites, ratings, comments, recommendations

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data:** Login times, pages viewed, features used (only if you consent to analytics)
- **Technical Data:** IP address (for security), browser type, device information
- **Subscription Data:** Stripe customer ID (no credit card details stored by us)

### 2.3 Information We Never Collect
- ❌ Credit card details (handled by Stripe)
- ❌ Sensitive health data beyond food preferences
- ❌ Location tracking
- ❌ Browsing history outside our app

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### 3.1 Essential Uses (Required for Service)
- **Account Management:** Create and maintain your account
- **Core Features:** Progress tracking, score calculation, recommendations
- **Security:** Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
- **Communication:** Send important service updates, password resets
- **Legal Compliance:** Fulfill legal obligations

**Legal Basis:** Contract performance (GDPR Article 6(1)(b))

### 3.2 Optional Uses (Require Your Consent)
- **Analytics:** Understand how features are used to improve the app
- **Marketing:** Send product updates, wellness tips, new features (you can opt-out anytime)

**Legal Basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

### 3.3 Legitimate Interests
- **Service Improvement:** Anonymous bug fixes and performance optimization
- **Research:** Anonymized statistics for wellness research (no personal data)

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

## 4. Your Privacy Rights

### 4.1 Access Your Data
You can view all your data in your account settings at any time.

### 4.2 Export Your Data
Download your complete data in JSON or CSV format from Settings → Privacy → Export Data.

**Included:** All progress data, recipes, meal plans, preferences, and more  
**Format:** Machine-readable JSON or human-readable CSV

### 4.3 Correct Your Data
Update your information anytime in Settings → Profile.

### 4.4 Delete Your Data
Request account deletion in Settings → Privacy → Delete Account.

**Process:**
- 30-day grace period before permanent deletion
- You can cancel deletion anytime within 30 days
- All data permanently removed after grace period
- Audit log maintained for legal compliance

### 4.5 Restrict Processing
Control how we use your data:
- Toggle analytics consent in Settings → Privacy
- Opt-out of marketing emails via Settings or unsubscribe link
- Disable specific features in your preferences

### 4.6 Object to Processing
You can object to:
- Analytics tracking (toggle off in settings)
- Marketing communications (unsubscribe anytime)
- Automated recommendations (dismiss in app)

### 4.7 Data Portability
Export your data in portable formats to transfer to another service.

## 5. How We Protect Your Data

### 5.1 Security Measures
- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Passwords:** Bcrypt hashing (never stored in plain text)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based permissions, session authentication
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and logging

### 5.2 Data Breach Protocol
If a breach occurs:
- We'll notify you within 72 hours if your data is affected
- Clear explanation of what happened
- Steps we're taking to fix it
- Actions you should take
- Contact for questions

### 5.3 Third-Party Security
All processors (Vercel, Stripe, database providers) are:
- GDPR compliant
- Certified secure (SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS)
- Bound by data processing agreements

---

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Active Accounts
- **Your Data:** Retained as long as your account is active
- **Control:** Delete anytime via account settings

### 6.2 Inactive Accounts (18+ Months)
- **Reminder Email:** Sent after 18 months of inactivity
- **Grace Period:** 30 days to log in and keep your account
- **Anonymization:** If no response, account is anonymized
  - Personal data deleted (name, email, password)
  - Progress logs removed
  - Anonymous statistics may be retained for research

### 6.3 Deleted Accounts
- **Permanent Deletion:** Within 30 days of deletion request
- **No Recovery:** Data cannot be recovered after deletion
- **Audit Log:** Deletion event logged for compliance (no personal data)

### 6.4 Legal Retention
- **Payment Records:** 7 years (tax/legal requirements)
- **Audit Logs:** 7 years (compliance)
- **Other Data:** Deleted as specified above

### 7.1 We Never Sell Your Data
Your personal data is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties.

- **Data Shared:** All app data (encrypted)

### 7.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose data if required by law:
- Court orders
- Legal investigations
- Protect rights and safety

You'll be notified unless legally prohibited.
